BIOGRAPHY

Tyler, the Creator, born Tyler Gregory Okonma, is an American artist known for his work as a rapper, singer, songwriter, and producer. He first gained attention in the early 2010s as a founding member of the music collective Odd Future.

Known for his creative and unique style, Tyler has continued to evolve his sound over his career. His projects often explore themes of identity and personal expression, while his artistic work extends into fashion, film, and graphic design.

With critically acclaimed albums like *Flower Boy* and the Grammy-winning *IGOR*, he has become a respected and influential figure in modern music.
